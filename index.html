How It Works

The app watches your phone's accelerometer. When it detects you were moving and then became stationary for 2+ minutes, it flags a potential toilet visit for you to confirm or dismiss.

⚠️ On iPhone, you'll be asked to grant motion permission when you enable tracking. HTTPS is required for motion data on mobile.
